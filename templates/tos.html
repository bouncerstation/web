Terms of service

  1. Use common sense. Don't be stupid, don't violate the ToS, and follow the rules of the network you are connecting to.
  2. Don't spam. This includes attempting a Denial of Service attack on the bouncer. Don't spam users on the network you're connecting to.
  3. Don't share bouncer credentials. Don't ask for them, and don't give them to other users.
  4. Bots are allowed, but we're not responsible for them. Servers know who you are. If your bot does a bad thing, you cannot blame us.
  5. Don't do illegal things. Follow the laws of your locality. Follow United States and California law as well.
  6. Be reasonable. Try to limit yourself to ten networks. If you need more, let us know. You can have an unlimited number of channels per network.
  7. One connection per network per account. This should be self-explanatory.
  8. Servers know who you are. We run an identd. Servers know your ZNC username.

Privacy policy

  1. We respect your privacy. We never share the information you give us with others (unless it is required by law).
  2. We don't read your logs. Although we do log all IRC traffic for your personal convenience, we never read these logs unless required by law.
  3. Access to servers is restricted. The ZNC server is a VPS hosted by Inertia Networks. Shell access is restricted to ZNC administrators.
  4. Your passwords are safe. We hash and salt your passwords before storing them. SSL is available for both IRC and webadmin.
