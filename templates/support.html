There are several different ways you can get in contact with us to request support.

On IRC

Bouncer Station

To request help on our own Bouncer Station network, just connect to irc.bouncerstation.com port 6667.
You will automatically be joined to #help and #chat. You can request a Bouncer Station account in #help.
NOTE: To request an account, you must use the Bouncer Station network.

Freenode

Staff are available in #bouncerstation on the Freenode network.
You cannot request automatic account creation here, but staff are happy to create it for you manually.



Via Email

You can email us at admin@bouncerstation.com.
You can request accounts, request BindHosts, or anything else that staff can do. Just be aware it might take staff a bit to respond.
